## Team Registration Steps

### Step 1: Create Accounts
1. Go to vexrobotics.com (VEX) or firstlegoleague.org (FLL)
2. Create team account under "Register New Team"
3. Select Vietnam as region
4. Enter organization details (RoboKids Vietnam)

### Step 2: Register Students
1. Add team members with full legal names
2. Upload recent photos (for badges)
3. Designate drivers and alternates
4. Assign roles (driver, coach, scout, builder)

### Step 3: Submit Documents
1. Download consent forms from parent portal
2. Collect signed forms from all participants
3. Upload scanned copies to registration system
4. Submit school enrollment verification

### Step 4: Pay Fees
1. Receive invoice via email
2. Payment options: Wire transfer, QR code
3. RoboKids Vietnam covers team fees
4. Keep payment receipt for records

### Step 5: Complete Training
1. All coaches complete certification
   - VEX: Free online training (2 hours)
   - FLL: Coach Certification Program (4 hours)
2. Student safety briefing
3. Competition rules test (students)

### Step 6: Robot Inspection Prep
1. Review inspection checklist
2. Pre-inspect robot before event
3. Prepare inspection portfolio
4. Have backup parts ready
