## Slide 4: Our Curriculum Overview
```
Progressive Learning Path

Beginner Level (Ages 6-10)
- Block-based programming (Blockly)
- Robot basics: movement, sensors
- Projects: Maze navigation, shape drawing

Intermediate Level (Ages 10-13)
- Advanced sensors & AI basics
- Python introduction
- Projects: Line follower, obstacle avoidance

Advanced Level (Ages 14-16)
- Competition prep (VEX/FLL)
- Real-world problem solving
- Team collaboration & engineering

Total: 80+ lessons across all levels
```
