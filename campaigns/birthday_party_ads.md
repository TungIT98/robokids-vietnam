# Facebook Ad Creative - Birthday Party Robotics

## Campaign Overview
- **Campaign Name:** RoboKids Birthday Party - Hanoi
- **Objective:** Lead generation (form submissions)
- **Target Audience:** Parents age 25-40 in Hanoi, interested in STEM/children activities
- **Budget:** 5,000,000 VND/month
- **Duration:** Ongoing until 20 leads achieved

## Targeting Specifications

### Facebook Target
- Location: Hanoi (specifically Cầu Giấy, Đống Đa, Ba Đình, Tây Hồ)
- Age: 25-45
- Parents with children age 6-16
- Interests: STEM education, robotics, kids activities, parenting

### Zalo Target
- Groups: Vietnamese parent groups (Hà Nội)
- Age: 25-45
- Parents with school-age children
