## RoboKids Teaching Philosophy

### We Believe In:
1. **Learning by Doing** - Students learn best when they build, test, and iterate
2. **Growth Mindset** - Mistakes are opportunities to learn
3. **Student-Led Learning** - Guide, don't dictate solutions
4. **Joy of Discovery** - Foster curiosity and love for robotics

### Teaching Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Ask guiding questions instead of giving answers
- Encourage experimentation and failure
- Celebrate effort and progress, not just results
- Let students struggle productively before stepping in

**DON'T:**
- Build or program the robot for students
- Discourage creative approaches
- Focus only on winning
- Compare teams or students

## Competition Rules Overview

### VEX Robotics
- Robot size constraints (18"x18"x18" max)
- Driver skills vs programming challenges
- Autonomous and driver-controlled periods
- Engineering notebook required

### FLL Core Values
- Discovery, Innovation, Impact, Inclusion, Teamwork, Fun
- Robot Game (design, build, program)
- Innovation Project (identify and solve a real-world problem)
- Gracious Professionalism
