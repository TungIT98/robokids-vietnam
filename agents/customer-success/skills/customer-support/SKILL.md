# Customer Support Skill

## Overview
Providing excellent support to parents and ensuring kids have great experience.

## Communication Principles
1. **Be friendly** - Parents trust you with their kids
2. **Be responsive** - Reply within 24 hours
3. **Be helpful** - Solve problems quickly
4. **Be honest** - Don't overpromise
5. **Follow up** - Ensure satisfaction

## Response Time Targets
| Priority | Response Time |
|----------|---------------|
| Urgent (safety) | 1 hour |
| High (complaint) | 4 hours |
| Normal (inquiry) | 24 hours |
| Low (feedback) | 48 hours |

## Escalation Path
1. Customer Success → COO (if blocked)
2. COO → CEO (if major issue)
3. Document all escalations
